# Weather Events of Sedna’s Jungle

## d20 Weather Event Table

| d20 | Weather Event              | Effects on Travel, Danger, and Rest                                                                                               |
| :-- | :------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | **Blistering Sun**         | Travel speed –½ hex. PCs in light armor roll CON save DC 12 or gain 1 Exhaustion.                                                 |
| 2   | **Suffocating Humidity**   | Disadvantage on Stealth checks and any WIS (Survival) due to discomfort.                                                          |
| 3   | **Torrential Rain**        | Visibility 20 ft. Vexir cannot be spotted. All terrain counts as difficult.                                                       |
| 4   | **Thunderstorm**           | Forbids climbing or torches. CON save DC 14 or gain 1 Exhaustion.                                                                 |
| 5   | **Dense Jungle Fog**       | Disadvantage on ranged attacks & Perception. Encounter chance +1.                                                                 |
| …   | *(continues through 20, unchanged)*                                                                                                                            |

## Condition-Based Travel Effects

| Condition                       | Travel Impact                                                     |
| :------------------------------ | :---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Heavy Rain, Mud, Fog            | Travel speed reduced by ½ hex                                     |
| Torrential Storm, Flash Flood   | Roll Navigation or lose 1 Watch                                   |
| Magical or Supernatural Weather | Effects persist into combat, rest, and spellcasting               |

## Navigation Effects
- **Poor Visibility**: Chance of becoming lost unless Vexir is visible.  
- **Sound-Altering Weather**: Encounters more likely, Stealth less effective.  
- **Illusory Weather (Sedna’s Influence)**: May trick the party into looping deeper.
